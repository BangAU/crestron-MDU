MOTORISED SHADES

Automated blinds and drapery add style, convenience, and comfort throughout a home, lowering utility bills and increasing the home’s value and security. Crestron’s motorized shades feature exclusive Digital Quiet Motor Technology for virtually silent operation with convenient one-touch control. And we’re so confident that our smart blinds and drapery are the most quiet, durable and reliable that we offer a Limited Lifetime Warranty.

LIGHTING

Crestron’s smart home lighting system uses Digital Addressable Lighting Interface (DALI) technology, so you can control lights individually or all at once, set ‘scenes’ at the push of a button, and program lights to automatically turn on when you enter a room. Elegant keypads combine custom lighting control with luxurious button feel and designer aesthetics to complement any décor.

AUDIO

Create listening zones that deliver different audio to different rooms simultaneously, and immerse yourself in pristine sound from Crestron’s high-performance speakers and amplifiers, precision-engineered for the optimal listening experience. Crestron Home also works with your favourite audio devices, so you can cater to every preference.

CLIMATE

Horizon® smart thermostats and multi-zone heating and cooling systems are part of the complete Crestron smart home solution. With flexible wiring options and Wi-Fi® communication, they’re equally simple to install for new construction or retrofit. Maximise efficiency by remotely configuring multiple thermostats simultaneously, then save and reuse your configurations. And because they use external temperature and humidity sensors, thermostats can be hidden in a central closet and controlled remotely.

VIDEO

Crestron DigitalMedia™ technology is the gold standard video processing and distribution platform, delivering the very best video quality, smoothest channel and source selection, and field-tested reliability. It removes the mess of wires, remotes and consoles, allowing users to select and play any game on any TV with one touch – in full 4K, with no lag time, and without any special wiring.

SECURITY

Crestron Home unifies security technologies to provide complete monitoring and control for total peace of mind: from security systems – including door locks, video intercom, cameras and alarms, to power and energy management designed to prevent service disruption. No matter what happens – storms, heat waves, equipment failures – your home will work exactly as it was designed to.
